About Art Revolution

Discover the story behind Timișoara's cultural transformation

Our Mission

Art Revolution is dedicated to showcasing the vibrant cultural events happening in Timișoara, Romania's European Capital of Culture. We believe in the power of art to connect communities and inspire change.

What We Do

We aggregate events from multiple sources across Timișoara and enhance them with AI-powered insights. Our platform helps you discover hidden cultural gems and stay informed about the city's artistic pulse.

Technology

Built with modern web technologies, Art Revolution uses advanced scraping techniques to gather real-time event data and AI enhancement to provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

The Team

We're a passionate group of developers and culture enthusiasts committed to making Timișoara's events more accessible. Our team works 24/7 to ensure you never miss an important cultural moment.
